## Current Story Threads

### Main Quest

#### Algox

The party was pulled into the conflict between Snowspeakers and Icespeakers after the avalanche on the mountain. They reached Snowscorn Peak, survived the collapse into Skyhall, and left with the Algox civil war still burning around them.

#### Lurker

The Coral Shards have drawn the party beneath forest and sea. After finding heat for Pinter's bathysphere, Poul recovered another shard from the Sunless Trench, keeping the ancient Lurker crown within reach and still deeply suspicious.

Related entries:

- [Week 12: Thawed Woods](logbook/week-12-thawed-woods.md)

#### Unfettered

The Crystal Trench led the party into the ancient spire and the machines beneath the ice. The top of the spire gave them a Coral Shard, while the derelict elevator and Quatryl Library revealed that the autonomous machines are still organized, numerous, and dangerous.
